I Love Float Trips

Tubing & Rafting & Canoeing & Kayaking

I love 'em all

Introduction

My name is Logan and I love float trips. My deep-seated love for lazily navigating, and sometimes recklessly hurtling myself down, the scenic lengths of winding bodies of water in various watercraft began long ago in a very Missouri childhood, not that far away. From tubing with Dad as a kid, to church youth group floats as a teenager, to partying on a raft full of goofballs in my 20s, to peacefully canoeing with the girlfriend in my 30s, to seeking out new rivers to explore with my family as I near my 40s, I've been at it for quite some time and don't see myself stopping anytime soon.

Some of the rivers I've floated thus far are:

  • Black River
  • Big River
  • Niangua River
  • Big Piney River
  • the North Fork of the White River
  • Buffalo River

I have also been on:

  • Meramec River
  • Jacks Fork
  • Current River
  • Eleven Point
  • Beaver Creek

These last five are my absolute favorite places to go floating. I have been to each of them at least four times. Every one of them has their own varied set of cool, as well as less than cool, things about them. I'll get into further detail regarding those details below!

Final Thoughts

Nomatter what time of year or what's happening in my life, the river always seems to be calling my name, and I always seem to be thinking about when I can get back to it again. Being at the river always brings back all the sweet memories I've made there over the years. It's also just a really great time and the best way to shed your worries and stress as you relax and drift downstream. Something about floating scratches a sort of explorers itch that I think we all have in us; some more than others. Though I've mostly floated on Missouri waterways, I do have my sights set on several other locations across the country. Heck, maybe someday I'll be able to take it to the next level and start floating on a global scale. Anyway, thank you for tuning into my river ramble.

Farewell & float on!
